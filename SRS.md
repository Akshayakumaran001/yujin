# Software Requirements Specification

## Functional Requirements
- Authentication (JWT)
- Role-based access
- Contact management
- Messaging + templates
- Queue-based sending
- Retry mechanism
- Logging & audit
- Kill switch
- Opt-out

## Non-Functional
- Security: JWT, validation
- Reliability: retries, logging
- Performance: ~200 msgs/day
- Maintainability: modular design
